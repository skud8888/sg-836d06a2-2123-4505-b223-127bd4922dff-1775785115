## Notes
Build mobile-friendly tool for trainers to capture evidence during courses: attendance photos, practical assessment photos, completed work samples, signed attendance sheets, safety checklists. Critical for compliance audits.

Evidence types:
- Attendance photo (group photo each day)
- Practical assessment (student performing task)
- Completed work (finished project/task)
- Documents (signed forms, checklists)
- Safety compliance (PPE, workspace setup)

Must support offline capture with sync when back online.
